The Future of Computer Hardware

Moore’s Law of the doubling of our computing power every year may not have been exactly right, but this prediction has some validity to it. Current technologies will be improved upon to become more condensed and faster, and new devices such as quantum computers and nanotech will result in an unprecedented amount of computing power. The future holds many uncertainties, but we can be sure of one thing, innovations in computer hardware will be at the center of our increasingly digital society.
